North America

North America is a vast continent in the Northern Hemisphere. Depending on the definition of North America, it can include from three to twenty-three countries. The most populated cities in North America are Mexico City, New York City, and Los Angeles. North America is famous for its Nature reserves such as Yosemite National Park in the United States, Banff National Park in Canada, and Izta-Popo Zoquiapan National Park in Mexico. Due to historical reasons, getting around in North America may prove difficult without a personal vehicle so it is recommended that travelers consider renting, borrowing, or buying a vehicle if they plan to visit multiple cities or national parks. The climate in North America differs greatly from region to region and as such proper planning should be undertaken when researching your trip. In many places within North America, safety is a major concern. Travelers should secure their possessions at all times when traveling and exercise heightened caution when visiting certain areas known to be unsafe. Some cities which make for good tourist destinations include New York City, Vancouver, and Mexico City. In the US and Canada, traveling can get very expensive. Travelers should have well thought-out budgets.
