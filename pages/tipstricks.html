Travel Tips and Tricks

Here is a list of curated travel tips and tricks from across the internet to help you on your travels:

  • Don't overpack
  • Use a credit/debit with no foreign transaction fees
  • Learn some phrases in the local language of the place you are visiting
  • Get vaccinated prior to travel. This includes the Covid-19 vaccine as well as vaccines for yellow fever.
  • Use travel insurance
