Europe

Europe is the continent with the highest rate of international tourism. Countries such as France, Spain, and Italy are among the most visited countries in the world. People from all over the world travel to Europe to see some beautiful sights, eat good food, and enjoy good company. Even within Europe, many Germans and Brits choose Southern Europe as a travel destination due to its comfortable climate and rich history. The Schengen Zone, which removed border controls between most countries of the European Union, allows for easy and affordable travel between many European Countries. Getting to Europe, finding hotels, and going sightseeing will not prove to be difficult for most travelers. Travelers should approach certain regions with caution such as Eastern Ukraine, border regions of the EU with Turkey or Belarus, and parts of Russia. Within Europe, travel between cities or even between countries can be done using public transport such as buses or trains. Air travel within Europe is also easy due to the presence of several low cost airlines which operate out of lesser known airports. Some countries which many travelers recommend include Italy, the Czech Republic, and Croatia. Good luck on your travels and be sure to leave this article a rating.
